Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about DevConf 2026? We've got answers.

Can I change my ticket plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade your ticket tier at any time before August 15, 2026, depending on availability. Just contact our support team.

Are the workshops included in the Standard pass?

The Standard pass includes access to 2 workshop sessions. For unlimited access to all hands-on workshops, you will need the Pro or Team pass.

Will the session recordings be available after the event?

Yes! Standard passes get digital access for 30 days post-event, while Pro and Team pass holders get lifetime access to all recorded talks.

Do you offer group or team discounts?

Yes, our Team package is explicitly discounted for groups up to 10 people. For larger corporate groups, please use the "Contact Us" option.
